about collection

Pimitive digs deep into the Naruto Shippuden archives to unearth seldom used artwork from the renowned anime. Standout pieces include a puffer jacket featuring Six Paths Naruto and a washed oversized t-shirt presenting Orochimaru complemented with a special treatment of Primitive lettering in rhinestones.

Historical series moments such as the Final fight between Naruto and Sasuke against Madara, are immortalized on t-shirts, hoodies, headwear, and accessories.
